TERRA VERA

Terra Vera is a pesticidal device formulated to effectively control microbes without compromising the integrity of a plant’s cannabinoid and terpene profile. Its underlying technology has been proven for 25 years in US EPA drinking water treatment facilities and now accessible to the agricultural industry.
